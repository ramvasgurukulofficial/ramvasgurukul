FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Reach out on WhatsApp if you need more help.

Is there a free demo class available? +

Yes! You can book a completely free demo class — no fees, no commitment — and experience Shivam Sir's teaching before enrolling. Visit the Book Free Demo page or WhatsApp / call +91 8700290436.

Which classes and subjects do you teach? +

Our primary focus is Class 9 & 10 CBSE (English medium) — Mathematics, Science, Social Science, English and Hindi.
Also available: Class 8 (all subjects), Class 11 & 12 (Physics, Chemistry, Maths) and Programming (C, C++, Python, Java, AI/ML).

Are classes online or offline? +

All classes are live online classes on Zoom, so students can join from anywhere in India. Every session is recorded and shared for revision after class.

Do you follow the NCERT and CBSE syllabus? +

Yes, all teaching is 100% NCERT-aligned and CBSE board exam focused — with chapter notes, NCERT solutions and board-pattern practice. ICSE and State Board students are also welcome.

What are the fees and batch timings? +

Fees depend on the class and subjects you choose. Classes run Monday–Sunday, 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM IST. WhatsApp or call +91 8700290436 for the current fee structure and available batch seats.

How do I join Ramvas Gurukul? +

Three simple steps: 1) Book a free demo class online or on WhatsApp. 2) Attend the live demo with Shivam Sir. 3) If you're happy, enroll in the Class 9 or Class 10 live batch and start learning.

Online tuition ki fees kitni hai? (What are the fees?) +

Fees aapki class aur subjects par depend karti hai. Class 9 aur Class 10 ke liye affordable online tuition available hai. Exact fees aur batch timings jaanne ke liye WhatsApp ya call karein +91 8700290436 par. Pehle ek free demo class bhi le sakte hain.

Haan! Ramvas Gurukul 100% online hai, isliye aapko "tuition near me" dhoondhne ki zaroorat nahi. India ke kisi bhi city ya gaon se students ghar baithe live online class join kar sakte hain — same quality teaching, bina travel ke. Bas ek phone ya laptop aur internet chahiye.
