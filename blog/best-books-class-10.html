Best Books for Class 10

Choosing the right books in Class 10 is very important because this year plays a crucial role in a student’s academic journey. The marks obtained in Class 10 board examinations often influence future opportunities and build the foundation for higher studies.

Many students feel confused about which books they should study from, but the key is not to study from too many books. Instead, students should focus on a few reliable books and study them thoroughly with proper understanding, revision, and practice.

NCERT – The Most Important Book

The most important book for every Class 10 student is the NCERT textbook. NCERT should always be the first priority because most board exam questions are directly or indirectly based on NCERT content.

Students should read every chapter carefully, understand the concepts, and solve all examples and exercise questions given in the book. Many important definitions, formulas, and explanations in board exams are taken directly from NCERT. Therefore, students should never skip any part of the NCERT textbook.

Mathematics Books

For mathematics, after completing NCERT, students should practice from RD Sharma. Mathematics requires consistent practice, and RD Sharma provides a large variety of questions that help students develop strong problem-solving skills.

The book contains questions of different difficulty levels that gradually improve a student’s understanding and logical thinking. Students should first understand the concept from NCERT and then solve additional practice questions from RD Sharma to strengthen their preparation.

Science Books

For science subjects such as physics, chemistry, and biology, the Pradeep book series is very useful. These books explain scientific concepts in a detailed and simple manner, which helps students understand complex topics easily.

Physics requires understanding formulas, concepts, and numerical problem solving. Chemistry focuses on chemical reactions, equations, and conceptual understanding. Biology includes theoretical concepts, diagrams, and important definitions.

By studying NCERT first and then practicing questions from Pradeep books, students can develop a strong understanding of science subjects.

English and Hindi

For English and Hindi, Arihant All-in-One books are helpful for preparation and revision. These books include chapter summaries, grammar exercises, and important questions that help students improve their language skills.

They also provide practice questions and sample answers that help students understand how to write answers properly in exams. Students can use Arihant All-in-One books to revise literature chapters and practice writing skills effectively.

Social Science

For Social Science, Arihant All-in-One is also a good reference book. Social Science includes history, geography, civics, and economics, which require both conceptual understanding and memorization.

The Arihant book provides simplified explanations, summaries, and important questions that help students revise chapters quickly. Practicing questions from this book also helps students improve their answer-writing skills.

Previous Year Questions (PYQ)

Another very useful resource for Class 10 preparation is chapterwise previous year questions (PYQ) from MTG. Practicing PYQ helps students understand the type of questions asked in board exams and the pattern of the examination.

When students solve chapterwise PYQ regularly, they become familiar with exam-level questions and improve their confidence.

Additional Study Material

In addition to these books, study materials provided by Ramvas Gurukul such as notes, daily practice problems (DPP), and question papers can be extremely helpful.

Ramvas Gurukul notes simplify difficult topics and provide clear explanations that help students revise quickly. Daily practice problems help students maintain regular practice, especially in subjects like mathematics and science.

Question papers help students test their preparation and improve time management during examinations.

Effective Study Strategy

To study effectively, students should follow a systematic approach. First, they should read the chapter from NCERT and understand the basic concepts.

After that, they should solve all the exercise questions from the NCERT textbook. Then they can practice additional questions from RD Sharma or Pradeep books depending on the subject.

After completing practice from reference books, students should solve chapterwise PYQ from MTG to understand exam-level questions.

Finally, they should revise the topic using Ramvas Gurukul notes and attempt practice question papers to evaluate their preparation.

Conclusion

Revision is also an essential part of preparation. Students should revise important formulas, definitions, and concepts regularly so that they remain fresh in memory.

Making short notes, highlighting key points, and solving practice questions frequently can help students retain information for a longer time.

In conclusion, the best study strategy for Class 10 students is to focus on a combination of NCERT textbooks, RD Sharma for mathematics, Pradeep for science subjects, Arihant All-in-One for languages and Social Science, and chapterwise PYQ from MTG for exam preparation.

When these books are combined with Ramvas Gurukul notes, daily practice problems, and question papers, students can build strong concepts, improve their practice, and perform well in board examinations.

Consistent study, disciplined practice, and regular revision are the key factors that lead to success in Class 10.
