How to Score 95+ Percent in Class 10

Scoring more than 95 percent in Class 10 is a goal for many students because the board examination is one of the most important milestones in a student’s academic life. Achieving such a high score requires discipline, proper planning, consistent effort, and the right study strategy.

Many students believe that studying for long hours automatically leads to high marks, but the truth is that the quality of study is more important than the number of hours spent studying. With the right approach, students can prepare effectively and achieve excellent results in their board examinations.

Build Strong Conceptual Understanding

The first and most important step to scoring high marks is building strong conceptual understanding. Students should focus on understanding the concepts rather than memorizing them without clarity.

When concepts are clear, students can easily solve questions even if they are asked in a slightly different way in the examination. Reading the NCERT textbook carefully is extremely important because most board exam questions are based on NCERT content.

Students should read each chapter thoroughly, understand the explanations, and solve all examples and exercise questions given in the book.

Practice Regularly

Regular practice is essential for subjects such as mathematics and science. These subjects require continuous problem solving to build accuracy and confidence.

Students should practice questions daily and solve different types of problems from reference books and previous year question papers. Regular practice helps students become familiar with question patterns and improves their problem-solving ability.

Effective Time Management

Time management is another important factor in achieving high marks in board exams. Students should create a well-planned study timetable that allows them to cover all subjects properly.

A balanced timetable ensures that every subject receives enough attention. Difficult subjects should be studied when the mind is fresh and active.

Importance of Revision

Revision is one of the most important aspects of exam preparation. Students should revise each chapter multiple times before the examination so that the concepts remain clear in their memory.

Making short notes and highlighting important points can help in quick revision during the final days before exams.

Solve Previous Year Question Papers

Practicing previous year question papers helps students understand the exam pattern, marking scheme, and the types of questions commonly asked in board exams.

When students solve question papers under timed conditions, they improve their speed and accuracy and identify weak areas that need improvement.

Improve Answer Writing Skills

Answer writing practice plays a significant role in scoring high marks. Students should practice writing structured answers with clear points, headings, and diagrams wherever required.

A well-presented answer sheet helps examiners evaluate answers easily and can improve marks.

Maintain a Healthy Routine

Students should get enough sleep, eat healthy food, and take short breaks between study sessions. A healthy routine helps maintain concentration and productivity.

Avoid Distractions

Mobile phones, social media, and unnecessary entertainment can waste valuable time. Limiting distractions during study hours helps students remain focused on their goals.

Stay Confident and Positive

Confidence and a positive mindset are essential during examinations. Students should trust their preparation and remain calm during the exam.

Conclusion

Scoring 95 percent or more in Class 10 is achievable with discipline, proper planning, regular practice, and consistent revision. When students stay focused, avoid distractions, and maintain confidence, they can achieve excellent results in their board examinations.
