Inside a Live Class

What a Live Interactive Class Looks Like

Every Class 9 & 10 session follows a clear structure — no time wasted, every minute planned.

Start
Quick warm-up & recap The class begins with a quick recall of the last topic, so today's concept connects to what students already know.
Teach
Concept with a real-life example Shivam Sir opens the new topic with a story or real-life example first — students understand before any definition is written.
Notes
NCERT coverage & clean notes The NCERT portion is covered line by line, definitions are dictated slowly, and diagrams are drawn together — everyone writes proper notes.
Practice
Board-pattern questions live Students solve MCQs, assertion–reason and exam-style questions in class, so the board paper never feels new.
End
Doubts + homework Every doubt is answered before the class ends, homework is assigned, and the recording plus notes are shared right after.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining your first class.

How do the live Zoom classes work? +

Classes are held live on Zoom at a fixed scheduled time. You join using a Zoom link shared by Shivam Sir, attend the interactive session, ask questions freely in real time, and receive the full class recording plus study material on Google Drive after each class.

What if I miss a class? +

Every class is recorded and shared with all enrolled students. If you miss a session for any reason, you can watch the full recording at your own time and stay completely on track.

Do I need to install anything special? +

You only need the Zoom app, which is completely free to download on Android, iOS, or PC. No other software, paid tools, or subscriptions are required.

Can students from outside Delhi join? +

Yes — all classes are 100% online via Zoom, so students from any city, town, or state in India can join without any issues. Location does not matter at all.

How many students are in each batch? +

Batches are kept small intentionally so that every student gets personal attention during live classes and dedicated time for doubt-solving. Shivam Sir ensures no one is left behind.
