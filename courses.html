What are the fees for Class 9 & 10 online coaching? +

Fees depend on whether you join the full all-subjects batch or single-subject tuition. WhatsApp or call +91 8700290436 for the current fee structure, batch timings and available seats.

Can I join for only one subject, like Maths or Science? +

Yes. You can join the full Class 9 or Class 10 batch for all subjects, or take single-subject online tuition — for example only Maths, only Science or only Social Science.

Yes. The coaching is designed for CBSE English-medium students. Concepts are explained in simple English and Hindi so nothing is left unclear.
