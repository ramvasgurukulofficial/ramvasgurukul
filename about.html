About the Tutor

Who is Shivam Sir?

Shivam Sir holds a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering and brings over 7 years of hands-on teaching experience helping students across Class 9 to 12 build genuine subject knowledge and score well in their board exams.

His teaching philosophy is rooted in one belief: if a student truly understands a concept, they can solve any problem — in the exam hall, and beyond. Every live session is interactive. Students are encouraged to question, explore, and think rather than passively listen and memorise.

Through Ramvas Gurukul, he blends the personal attention of a traditional Gurukul with the reach of modern technology — live Zoom classes, recorded lectures, weekly tests, and WhatsApp support — so every student across India gets the same quality of guidance.
