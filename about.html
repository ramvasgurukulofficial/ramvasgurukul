About the Tutor

Who is Shivam Sir?

Shivam Sir holds a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering and brings over 7 years of hands-on teaching experience. His main focus is Class 9 & 10 CBSE (English medium) — the two years that decide the board result — where he has helped 500+ students build genuine subject knowledge and score well.

His teaching philosophy is rooted in one belief: if a student truly understands a concept, they can solve any question — in the exam hall, and beyond. Every live session is interactive. Students are encouraged to question, explore, and think rather than passively listen and memorise.

Unlike big coaching apps, there are no rotating or junior teachers here. Shivam Sir teaches every batch, checks every test personally, and shares each student's progress with parents on WhatsApp. Through Ramvas Gurukul, he blends the personal attention of a traditional Gurukul with modern tools — live Zoom classes, recordings, weekly tests and WhatsApp support.

Teaching Method

How Shivam Sir Teaches

The same four-step method in every Class 9 & 10 live class — designed for real understanding and board exam marks.

🎯

1. Concept First, Marks Follow

Every topic starts with a real-life example, so the student truly understands the concept before writing any definition — no blind memorisation.

📖

2. NCERT Line by Line

The complete NCERT is covered in simple English and Hindi, with clean chapter notes. Nothing in the CBSE syllabus is skipped.

✍️

3. Board-Pattern Practice

MCQs, assertion–reason, case-based and long-answer questions — practised exactly the way they appear in the CBSE board paper.

🔁

4. Test, Feedback, Improve

Weekly tests are personally checked by Shivam Sir, and each student's progress is shared with parents on WhatsApp.
