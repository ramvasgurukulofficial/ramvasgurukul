About the Tutor

Ramvas Gurukul is guided by Shivam, an educator committed to helping students build strong academic foundations.


He holds a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering and has 7 years of teaching experience. With a deep passion for teaching, he focuses on explaining concepts in a clear and simple manner so that students can understand their subjects with confidence.


His teaching approach emphasizes conceptual clarity, regular practice, and disciplined learning. The goal is not only to help students perform well in exams but also to help them develop a deeper understanding of the subjects they study.


Through Ramvas Gurukul, he aims to create a supportive and focused learning environment where students can ask questions freely, strengthen their knowledge, and achieve their academic goals.

About Ramvas Gurukul

Ramvas Gurukul is dedicated to providing quality education and helping students build strong academic foundations. Our aim is to make learning simple, clear, and effective so that students truly understand their subjects and perform confidently in exams.


At Ramvas Gurukul, we believe that strong concepts, regular practice, and proper guidance are the keys to success. Our teaching approach focuses on explaining topics in an easy and practical way so that every student can learn at their own pace and improve steadily.


We provide structured lessons, important questions, and exam-oriented preparation to support students in their academic journey. Our goal is not only to help students score good marks but also to develop a deep understanding of the subjects they study.


Ramvas Gurukul follows the traditional values of a Gurukul—discipline, dedication, and continuous learning—combined with modern teaching methods to create a positive and effective learning environment for students.


At Ramvas Gurukul, we are committed to guiding students toward academic success and a brighter future.
